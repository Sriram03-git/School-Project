Our Facilities

Designed for safety, comfort, and inspiration.

A spacious, fenced outdoor area with child-safe equipment like slides, swings, and sandpits. Soft flooring ensures injury-free play.

When the sun is too hot, our air-conditioned indoor playroom provides a cozy space for soft play, puzzles, and group activities.

Reading Corner

A quiet, carpeted corner filled with colorful picture books to ignite the love for reading from a young age.

Art Studio

Where little Picassos are born! A messy-allowed zone for painting, clay modeling, and crafting.
