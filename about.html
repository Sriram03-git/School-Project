About Arasi

Founded in 2007, Arasi Nursery And Primary School has been dedicated to nurturing young minds with love, care, and excellence.

Our Mission

To provide a safe, stimulating, and inclusive environment where every child is encouraged to explore, discover, and develop their full potential. We strive to foster a lifelong love for learning through play and creativity.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of early childhood education, raising a generation of kind, creative, and confident individuals who contribute positively to the world.
